| Self-balancing vehicle control | [Joystick](./输入-人机-摇杆) + [Rotary Encoder](./输入-人机-旋转编码器) |

## Common Knowledge

- **Debouncing is mandatory**: hardware RC or software delay (5~30 ms)
- **Long-press / double-click**: state machine for multi-function recognition
- **Matrix scanning**: scan period around 10 ms
- **Pull-up / pull-down**: floating inputs are noise-sensitive
- **Industrial anti-interference**: TVS, shielded cable, level shifting
