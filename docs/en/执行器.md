# Actuators Overview

Actuators convert electrical signals into physical action. DriverKit documents **11 actuator modules**, organized into **five fields**: Motion Motors, Switching, Fluid, Light Source, and Sound / Light.

## Common Knowledge

- **Driver stage is required** — motors MUST NOT be wired directly to an MCU.
- **Freewheeling diode / dead-time**: H-bridge shoot-through and inductive-load protection.
- **Thermal design dictates lifetime**: keep power-device temperature rise < 80°C.
- **Closed-loop vs open-loop**: precision applications use encoder motors with closed-loop control.
- **PWM frequency**: motors 1~25 kHz; LEDs > 200 Hz to avoid visible flicker.
